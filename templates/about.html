About Our Platform

Diabetic Retinopathy

Diabetic Retinopathy is a diabetes complication that affects eyes. It's caused by damage to the blood vessels of the retina and can lead to blindness if untreated. Early detection is crucial.

Pneumonia

Pneumonia is an infection that inflames the air sacs in one or both lungs. It can be caused by bacteria, viruses or fungi. Detection through chest X-rays and AI models speeds up diagnosis.

Lung Cancer

Lung cancer begins in the lungs and is one of the leading causes of cancer deaths. Early diagnosis through CT scans and AI-based pattern recognition increases survival chances.

Diabetes

Diabetes is a chronic health condition that affects how your body turns food into energy. Monitoring and prediction of blood sugar trends help manage and prevent complications.

Parkinson's Disease

Parkinson's is a progressive nervous system disorder that affects movement. AI can assist in early symptom detection through voice, hand tremor, and gait analysis.

Heart Disease

Heart disease involves narrowed or blocked blood vessels. Early detection through ECG, blood tests and AI analysis can help prevent heart attacks and improve treatment outcomes.
